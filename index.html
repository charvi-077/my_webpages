Namaste !
I do what I love, I love what I do

I am MS student at UC Santa Barbara and graduate researcher at the Vision Research Lab, advised by Prof. B. S. Manjunath. My interests lie at the intersection of 2D/3D computer vision and robotics, focusing on systems that can see, understand, and interact with the world through visual intelligence and geometric reasoning.

Before UCSB, I was a Research Associate at IIIT Hyderabad, advised by Dr. Harikumar Kandath and Dr. Ravi Kiran, and previously worked as a Robotics Software Engineer at Flux Auto, Inc, an Intern at BlackCoffeeRobotics, and as an Outreachy Developer Intern at Git. I combine research with engineering skills to solve real-world vision problems across robotics, geospatial analysis, and scientific imaging. I am open to opportunities and collaborations in 2D/3D computer vision and always happy to chat if you’re exploring similar ideas.

You can find my resume here.

Research Interests : SLAM, 3D reconstruction, Gaussian Splatting, 3D Generative Models, VLM, Object Detection/Tracking, Segmentation
